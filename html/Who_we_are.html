Who we are

Legacy

The RoughNeck story is a truly remarkable one. We started as a rubber balloon factory with a funding of Rs.14, 000 way back in the 90’s is now a multibillion legacy that produces quality tyres used all around India & internationally along with a presence in paints & coats, toys, motorsports and cricket training

Origin

RoughNeck’s origin traces back to the humble shack in Madras that housed its first makeshift toy balloon manufacturing unit set up in 1996. It was not until 2002 when it changed course and turned to tread rubber manufacturing. Thus began its glorious reign as the undisputed leader in the tyre making industry

Recognition

RoughNeck is recognized for its drive towards continuous quality improvement and customer satisfaction. It has won the JD Power award not once but 10 times till date. It has also won the TNS and CAPEXIL awards for being voted as the most trusted tyre company in India.
